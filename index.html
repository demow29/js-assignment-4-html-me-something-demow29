Thrift Shop Art Collection

Over the years, I have collected many artifacts from thrift shops. These pieces are less than extraordinary. I have little context on the artist and time these works were concieved. These works hang on my bedroom wall with no inherent sentiment attached to them. If I had to give advice to an amphibious creature from another planet on how to be human, I would tell them to start by forming an uncontrived appreciation literally anything.


Painting

Found at Goodwill Outlet
St. Louis, Missouri
Description: A watercolor style painting shows a yellow beach with two seagulls painted with blues and purples. One stands round ball of netting with its wings up while the other picks at a frayed rope attached to the netting.

Photo

Found at Saint Vincent De Paul
St. Louis, Missouri
Description: A black and white photo showing an exterior arched doorway. Carved into the top of the stone arch is a bell surrounded by botanicals. A human gargoyle sits on either side of the arch

The Betrothal

Found at Saint Vincent Depaul
St. Louis, Missouri
Description: A woman wearing a long bonnet and apron dances jovially with a man in a brimmed hat and green jacket. A banner at the bottom reads, Betrothal Dance, 'Evangeline'

Found at Goodwill Outlet
St. Louis, Missouri
Description: Two angels with butterfly like wings hold a net that has capture a fish with a human-esque face. A large bird with skinny, long legs and a rounds body stands behind the figures. Below a block of orange, a man in a uniform sits on a horse.

Zig Zag Woman

Found at Goodwill Outlet
Saint Louis, Missouri
Description: A woman made of geometrical blocks of purple, blue, and peach colors sits with her arms resting on a table staring downward.
